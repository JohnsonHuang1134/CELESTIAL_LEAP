E-Commerce Website Development FAQs.

What is E-Commerce Website Development?

E-commerce website development involves the process of creating a website specifically designed to sell products or services online. It involves various aspects such as website design, shopping cart integration, payment gateway integration, and security measures to ensure safe and secure transactions.

An e-commerce website allows businesses to reach a wider audience beyond the geographical boundaries of a traditional brick-and-mortar store. It offers customers the convenience of shopping for products or services at their own convenience, regardless of time and location.

The end goal of e-commerce website development is to create a user-friendly, secure, and efficient online shopping environment that enhances the customer experience, drives sales, and fosters business growth.

What services are included in E-Commerce Website Development Services?

E-Commerce Website Development Services typically encompass a range of services and features designed to create a comprehensive and efficient online shopping platform. Here are some of them:

– Website Design and Development
– Shopping Cart Integration
– Payment Gateway Integration
– Data Security
– Mobile Responsiveness
– SEO
– Data Analytics and Reporting
– Integration with CRM and ERP Systems

What features are included in E-Commerce Website Development Services?

Key features of an e-commerce website often include

– Product catalogs
– Customer reviews and ratings
– Secure checkout processes
– Customer service features such as live chat, FAQs, email support and contact forms

What's the scope of customization available for my new website?

Creating a bespoke website tailored to your specific needs is entirely achievable. Every aspect – from features and functionalities to design aesthetics – can be crafted based on your individual needs and suggestions.

How much time will it take in E-Commerce Website Development?

The development timeline for standard websites typically spans around 55 – 75 working days. However, for custom websites, the duration is more variable and can only be determined after a detailed discussion of the project scope and requirements.

Do you provide post-development support for the website?

Indeed, we offer consistent website maintenance and support services. You can choose from our array of monthly packages to ensure your site remains well-maintained. Our team is always ready to assist with the integration of new functionalities, routine maintenance, and regular updates, according to your specific needs.

Do you provide training on managing and using my website once it's developed?

Absolutely. We provide comprehensive training and guidance to help you understand the nuances of website management. This includes instructions on how to efficiently use your website, and update content and images on your own.
